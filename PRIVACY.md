# Privacy Policy (draft)

TradePulse Alerts is a local-first app. We do not require accounts and we do not sell personal data.

## Data collection
- **We do not collect personal information** such as name, email, phone number, or precise location.
- **We do not run analytics or ads** in the current version.

## Data stored on your device
The app stores the following data locally on your device to function:
- Your settings (watched symbols, thresholds, polling/realtime preferences, quiet hours).
- Local alert history and recent price history for calculations.

You can delete this data by uninstalling the app (or clearing app storage).

## Notifications
If you enable notifications, the app may request permission from your operating system to send local alerts.

## Third-party services
The app fetches public market data from Bybit endpoints:
- `https://api.bybit.com/`
- `wss://stream.bybit.com/`

Network requests include the symbols you choose to monitor.
