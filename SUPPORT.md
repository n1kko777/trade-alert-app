## Frequently Asked Questions

Visit our FAQ page: https://tradealert.ru/faq

---

## Bug Reports

Found a bug? Email us at support@tradealert.ru with subject line "Bug Report" and include:
- Device model and OS version
- App version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots if applicable

---

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for improvement? We'd love to hear it! Email us at support@tradealert.ru with subject line "Feature Request".

---

## Subscription Issues

For subscription-related issues (billing, refunds, cancellation):
- **App Store:** Contact Apple Support or manage in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
- **Google Play:** Contact Google Play Support or manage in Google Play Store > Subscriptions
- **RuStore:** Contact RuStore Support or manage in RuStore app
